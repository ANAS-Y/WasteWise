WASTE CLASSIFIER

A Waste Classifier is a system used to categorize waste into different types, enabling efficient waste management and disposal. By accurately classifying waste, Waste Classifiers help reduce contamination, increase recycling rates, and promote sustainable waste management practices.
Recyclable Materials
Materials that can be processed and transformed into new products, such as paper, plastic, glass, and metal.
Organic (Biodegradable waste)
Biodegradable waste, including food waste, yard trimmings, and other natural materials that can be composted
Non-Recyclable Waste
Waste that cannot be recycled or composted, such as hazardous waste, textiles, and ceramics, which require specialized disposal methods.

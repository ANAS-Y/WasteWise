RECYCLING TIPS

Tip 1: Separate Waste

Always separate your waste into recyclables and non-recyclables. This makes the recycling process more efficient.

Tip 2: Clean Your Recyclables

Ensure that your recyclables are clean and free from food residue. This helps in preventing contamination.

Tip 3: Know What to Recycle

Learn about the items that can and cannot be recycled in your area. Not all plastics, for example, are recyclable.

Tip 4: Reduce and Reuse

Before recycling, think about how you can reduce waste and reuse items. This is even better than recycling.

Tip 5: Compost Organic Waste

Compost your organic waste to reduce the amount of waste going to landfills and create nutrient-rich soil for gardening.

Tip 6: Educate Others

Spread the word about the importance of recycling and proper waste management to help others make a positive impact.
